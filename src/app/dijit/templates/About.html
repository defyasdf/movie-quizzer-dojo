A Movie Guessing Game

This is a movie guessing game: You'll get the tagline to the movie and four options to choose from. If you need a hint, you will get additional information such as year released, director and top actors of the movie. All movie information is pulled dynamically from Google Freebase.

This game is a sample to showcase the combination of the Dojo framework, Intern unit testing, and jsdoc documentation generation.
